5 1/4 inch floppy pinout

Pinouts >  HDD and other storage devices connectors  >  obsolete computers pinouts

34 pin male EDGE connector
  • Ask a question
  • Comment
  • Edit
  • Submit New
for PC compatible drives

 

pin 34 is /READY (active LOW)

All pins on the other side are ground.

Pinout is correct for TRS-80 & most CP/M Computers. Pin 2 is REDWC* Reduced Write Current.  0=Double Density and 1=High Density.  That will update to the newer style Floppy Drives.

Pinout status: +2 -0
According to 4 reports in our database (2 positive and 0 negative) the 5 1/4 inch floppy pinout should be correct.
