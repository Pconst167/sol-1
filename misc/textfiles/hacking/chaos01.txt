 A> This is a cheap long distance service that has no whatsoever tracing
    except for the indept hack attemp logging if you are on for longer
    then 30 seconds or so I have heard.

 Q> Formats
 A> (Assuming the S8=7) 18002255946,,,10-DIGIT CODE,ACN

 Q> Format for code.
 A> 50316xxxxx   Easy as hell to hack!

  To hack this system you do not need a hacker yet it might help you a slight
  bit to save time.  Load up the hack with templates such as:

     5031635600 and scan to 5031635700

   (Note: Do not change the 50316 part of the code.. that stays the same.)

     Hack at will. No longer then 2hrs at a time, though I have hacked 8hrs
     straight and gotton over 500 codes that will last me till the system
     goes down.
