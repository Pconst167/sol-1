                      SOME PROGRAMMES AT A GLANCE
                      ---------------------------

NEWSLINE COLOGNE - a current affairs programme with reports, interviews,
                   a press review and comment on major developments
ECONOMIC NOTEBOOK - a weekly look at the economic scene in Germany and
                    around the world
INSIGHT - an in-depth feature giving the background to political events
          and developments on the international scene
LIVING IN GERMANY - a weekly look at the social scene in Germany
SPOTLIGHT ON SPORT - a weekly magazine programme with background stories
                     and coverage of important events
MAN AND ENVIRONMENT - a weekly programme on all topics relating to the
                      environment in industrial and developing countries
INTERNATIONAL TALKING POINT - a weekly round-table discussion on major
                              trends and events with journalists from
                              different countries
SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY - a weekly magazine programme presenting new
                         developments in science and technology
RELIGION AND SOCIETY - a weekly round-up of news and developments
                       concerning the world's major religions
PANORAMA - a review of the major events of the week on the international
           news scene and in Germany
DEVELOPMENT FORUM - a programme with reports and interviews on projects
                    and progress in Africa and Asia
ARTS ON THE AIR - reports and interviews on major cultural events and
                  developments including coverage of cultural relations
                  between Germany and the regions of Africa, Asia and
                  North America
HALLO AFRICA - a programme with musical requests and greetings to
               friends
AFRICA REPORT - a programme of reports and background to the news from
                Africa by Deutsche Welle correspondents
AFRICA THIS WEEK - a weekly review of trends and events on the African
                   continent
AFRICA HIGHLIGHT - a weekly feature on an important topic concerning
                   Africa
MONDAY SPECIAL - an interview or report on an event or development with
                 special relevance for Africa from the world of politics,
                 economic affairs or culture
POP FROM GERMANY - a weekly programme for listeners in Africa presenting
                   the German pop scene
AFRICA IN THE GERMAN PRESS - a weekly look at what German papers and
                             weeklies have to say about Africa
ASIA-PACIFIC REPORT - a programme of correspondents' reports, interviews
                      and background news from the Asia-Pacific region
LETTER FROM BERLIN/BONN - the tale of two cities - an old capital and a
                          new one - as seen by our correspondents
RANDOM SELECTION - a weekly programme for listeners in North America in
                   which Larry Wayne takes a look at Germany from the
                   lighter side
TO THE TOP - a music programme in which listeners in North
             America are brought up to date with the German pop scene
GERMAN BY RADIO - learning the German language made easy through our
                  weekly language course
EUROPEAN JOURNAL - a review of major events in europe and Germany through
                   interviews, analyses and background reports
MAILBAG - a programme produced in three different versions for Africa, Asia
          and North America in which listeners' mail is answered in a light
          and entertaining fashion
PHONE-IN - our North America team talks to listeners in the USA and Canada
TRANSATLANTIC DIARY - a look at cultural, science, economic and bilatera|
                      developments for North American listeners
