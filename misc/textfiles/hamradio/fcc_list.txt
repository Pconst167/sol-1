THE R.E.C/N6UII LIST OF FREQUENCY ALLOCATIONS FROM 60Khz to 40 GHz. 

VERSION 1.2

This list represents our achievment in radio communications over the years.
If you have allways wondered (hmmm...I wonder what frequency THATS on??), 
this is the list for you. Most every radio frequency generating service is
represented, some have been condensed or excluded for space limitations and
moral reasons. Due to the space limitations, many of the US government 
systems operating between 30 and 500mhz have been excluded.  Each frequency
will have two-letter codes indicating what service operates on that 
frequency.  Those 2 letter codes can be decoded below. After some of the
2 letter codes there may be some characters in brackets or paraenthsis, this
denotes an official or unofficial channel number for that frequency. 

A lot of painstaking time, a lot of research, a lot of back and head aches,
and a lot of ADVIL went in to making this list.  If you feel that you have
benefited from the information in this list, please send a small donation
to help make up for the time. The donation tells me that the computer world
appreciates the information I am providing and that they are looking forward
to updates of this list and future textfiles of interest from REC.
