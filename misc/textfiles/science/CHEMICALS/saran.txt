===========================================================================
                   Precautions for Safe Handling and Use
===========================================================================
Steps If Matl Released/Spill: SWEEP UP AND TRANSFER TO AN APPROPRIATE
CONTAINER FOR DISPOSAL.
Neutralizing Agent: NOT APPLICABLE.
Waste Disposal Method: BURN IN AN INCINERATOR EQUIPPED TO HANDLE HYDROGEN
CHLORIDE FUMES OR BURY IN AN APPROVED LANDFILL. PROCEDURES MUST BE COMPLY
WITH LOCAL, STATE AND FEDERAL REGULATIONS.
Precautions-Handling/Storing: STORE IN A COOL,VENTILATED AREA AWAY FROM
INCOMPATIBLE PRODUCTS.
Other Precautions: RESIN DEGRADES VERY SLOWLY EVOLVING HCL.KEEP IT AS COOL
AS POSSIBLE.RESIN IS MOST FREQUENTLY USED IN COMBINATION WITH FLAMMABLE
SOLVENT.TRANSFERRING OF RESIN FROM CONTAINER CAUSES STATIC BUILD UP WHICH
MAY CAUSE FIRE NEAR FLAMMABLE SOLVENT.
