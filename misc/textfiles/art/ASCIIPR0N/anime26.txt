This first edition of the Ranma 1/2 ASCii Art Collection contains 23 images.
 Most of these were drawn by me but there are a couple done by other folks
 too.  I'd like to have a greater percentage of work by other people in
 the next edition which will be out whenever I have enough stuff to release
 other edition.  In the meantime, I hope you enjoy this collection.

"Ranma 1/2" and characters relating to "Ranma 1/2" are copyright 1994
 Rumiko Takahashi and Shogakukan, Inc.
