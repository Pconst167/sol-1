                         New Weapons For TORG

The column headings are as follows:
Tech    Technological level
R1      Rounds fired per second on full automatic.
DV      Damage value of one round.
aDV     Damage value of a burst.  See variant autofire rules.
Clip    Rounds in clip.
Min     Minimum range in metres.
Shrt    Short range in metres.
Med     Medium range in metres.
Long    Long range in metres.
Price   Price in US$.
Value   Value on Torg scale.

The numbers are taken from  Phoenix  Command  (3rd ed.); I've adjusted
the ranges so that they match the  weapons  in the books a bit better.
Note that some of the DVs are changed (e.g. MAC-10 and Uzi).
