calls, person to person call, etc.  There are some 3, 7, & 9 lined loops.
These are very rare.  To use a loop, one person must call the 1000hz line.
The other must call the second line.  If the loop(s) work, the two will be
connected.  Here's an Example:

   One Person calls: 213-888-9900
   The other Calls : 213-888-9901
