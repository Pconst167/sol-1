The Occult and Metaphysical Business Directory
The past decade has seen a substantial increase in the number of
people involved in alternative religions and spirituality.
Consequently, there has been a boom in the number of businesses
catering to the needs of this growing community. Such businesses
are often small, and unable to afford advertising, relying upon
word of mouth to let people know of their existence.

I have been in the occult business for several years, and I
understand the important role such businesses play, not only as
suppliers of goods and services, but also as contact points for the
magical communities they serve. I am hoping that this directory
will help people find such contacts, as well as help to support the
growing occult and metaphysical business community.

Jennifer Holding
Editor


How to Use this directory
The addresses in this directory were collected from various
sources; other directories, advertisements, word of mouth, etc. I
set out to limit the directory to retail occult/metaphysical
stores. I have not included P.O. boxes, as cottage-industry-type
businesses could fill up a whole other directory. For the same
reason, I have not included women's book-stores, unless they
specialize in books on women's spirituality. Once the addresses
were collected, I sent questionnaires to each business to verify
the information, and to find out exactly what kinds of goods and
services they offered. Because not all of the businesses responded,
this information is not included in many of the listings. 

I have done my best to include only current addresses and phone
numbers. Unfortunately, the occult business is a risky one and some
of the businesses listed may no longer exist. If you discover this
to be the case, or you have more information, please let me know.
I have included a copy of the directory listing form in the back.

A complete listing includes the business name, address, phone
number, and a letter code describing the types of goods and
services offered by that business. The code translates as follows:-


Books:     O = Occult/Pagan/Women's spirituality
           M = Metaphysical/New Age
           E = Eastern religions/Spirituality

Supplies:  T = Tarot cards/ crystal balls/runes
           C = Candles/oils/occult supplies
           J = Jewellery/crystals/ritual tools
           H = Herbs/herbal preparations

Services:  R =Tarot/palm/psychic readings
           S = Seminars/classes
           P = Pagan contact/networking
           W = Wholesale/mail-order

These are general categories only, and a store may only offer one
or two of the goods or services under each category.
