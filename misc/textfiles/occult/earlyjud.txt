This is the composite bibliography from R.A. Kraft and G.W.E. Nickelsburg,
eds., _Early Judaism and Its Modern Interpreters_ (Atlanta/Philadelphia:
Scholars/Fortress, 1986) pp. 31-116 (= "Part One: Early Judaism in Its
Historical Settings"). I have attempted to make this as error-free as
possible, but it is the nature of these things that errors remain. Please
report to me any that you note.
 
The entries in this version have been arranged into records (separated by
##), and each record into fields. These are derived from, but not identical
to, the Ibid. system (from Dragonfly Software, makers of Nota Bene). This is
a listing of field tags with their meaning:
 
AU - Author
YR - Year of publication
ST - Short Title (any title enclosed by "...")
BT - Book/Long work Title (an italicized title)
*T - 2nd and 3rd lines for ST and BT
JR - Journal title
CO - Contributors (editors, translators)
DE - Descriptive identifier of ST, BT (dissertation, edition, etc.)
SR - Series
PL - Place of publication
PR - Press/publisher
V# - Volume number
PG - Pages (first and last for ST)
AN - Annotations (very rare in this list)
 
This setup has meant some compromises, but the alternative was to add a
number of fields. This is complex enough already!
 
Again, suggestions and corrections to:
 
David Reimer, Wilfrid Laurier University
