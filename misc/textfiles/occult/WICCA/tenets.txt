                                The Tenets
                                ----------

        Reincarnation
        Learning
        Balance
        Harmony
        Love 
        Trust
        Humility
        Tolerance

        ---
        -These have become common knowledge in neopaganism, at least
         in Ottawa. 
        -They don't seem to be part of the Gardneroid stream originally,
         but some Gardneroid traditions have adopted them.
        -Traceable sources include Sybil Leek's "The Complete Art of
         Witchcraft" (the only published source known to me)
         and the Wiccan Church of Canada (or so I have been told)
