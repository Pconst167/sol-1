TENNIS BALL FOUNTAIN

OK A FOUNTAIN IS A DEVICE WHICH SPRAY SPARKS OR SHOOTS SPARKS UP INTO THE 
AIR. IN THIS FILE YOU WILL LEARN HOW TO MAKE A TENNIS BALL FOUNTAIN-FIREWORK 
MINE.


*(EQUIPMENT)*- WHAT YOU NEED
1 TENNIS BALL
1 KING SIZE MATCH BOX (IF YOU CANT FIND KING SIZE BUY 2 REGULAR MATCH BOXES)
7 INCHES OF CANNON FUSE
DUCK TAPE
1 KNIFE
1 SCISSOR


*(WHAT TO DO)*- HOW TO USE THEM

FIRST OFF GET YOUR TENNIS BALL (PERFABLY BRAND NEW OUT OF THE PACKAGE) THEN 
START CUTTING THE MATCH HEADS OFF A #*@!LOAD OF MATCHES ENOUGH TO FILL THE 
TENNIS BALL AFTER THATS DONE PUT A SLIT IN THE TENNIS BALL WHICH CAN BE DONE 
WITH A SHARP KNIFE. START FILLING UP THE TENNIS BALL WITH MATCHES TRY TO 
PACK THEM TIGHT IN THE BALL.GET THE SCISSORS AND CUT 7 INCHES OF CANNON FUSE 
PUT IT IN THE BALL TRY TO SHOVE IT DOWN AS FAR AS YOU CAN WITHOUT DAMAGING 
THE FUSE PACK A LITTLE BIT MORE THEN DUCK TAPE THE BALL PRETTY TIGHT ALL 
AROUND NOT TOO MUCH DO IT NEAR THE SLIT TO WRAP IT AROUND LIKE 10 TIMES 
STOP. YOU ARE DONE ALL YOU HAVE TO DO NOW IS LIGHT THE FUSE. I RECCOMEND 
DOING THIS IN A EMPTY PARKING LOT WHERE THERE IS NO FLAMMABLE STUFF TO CATCH 
ON FIRE WHEN THIS THING GOES OFF. WHAT SHOULD HA#*@! WHEN IT REACHES INSIDE 
THE TENNIS BALL ALL IT SHOULD SPARK FOR A LIL BIT THEN ALL THE MATCHES WILL 
BE SHOOT UP INTO THE AIR STILL LIT ITS COOL WHEN YOU DO IT AT NIGHT. 
PROBALLY TO MAKE IT GO HIGHER PUT A LITTLE BIT OF GUN POWDER AT THE BOTTOM 
FOR A LIFT CHARGE. IT MAY TAKE A LONG TIME BUT ITS WORTH IT. HINT: THE 
TIGHTER YOU WRAP IT MORE SPARKS WILL COME OUT BUT IT WONT SHOOT INTO THE AIR
