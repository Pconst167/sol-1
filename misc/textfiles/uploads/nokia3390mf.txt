[1. How do I Set the Time?]
Press Menu, go to Settings, go down to Clock, press Select. 
Select Set the Time, insert the time, press ok, select am or pm, press ok.
Your clock should now be set.

[2. How do I assign tones?]
Press Menu, Select Phonebook, under the options go to the bottom and select
"Assign Tone", press select. Select the person in your phonebook you wish to
modify the tone, press assign. Select the tone you wish to use, and press OK.
Done!

[3. How do I clear call lists?]
On the Menu, select Call Log. Press down until you get to Clear Call Lists, 
press select. Select the menu you wish to clear press ok. There will be no
warnings.
