  a lighter

  water bottle

  gasoline

  tissue
  ----------------------------------------------------------
  instructions:

  empty out the water bottle and put about 4 teaspoons of gasoline in it

  rip the tissue in half, crumple that part up and soak it in some gas, put it in the of the bottle
  and shake it up thoroughly and light the tip of the tissue paper {its okay to hold it in your hand but i would recomend wearing gloves}
  -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  effect:

  the effect will be steady stream of fire shooting out of the bottle about a foot out
