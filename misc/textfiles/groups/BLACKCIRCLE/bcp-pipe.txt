             Well here is how you make a Pipe Bomb. All you 
          Need is some Pyrodex (or Gunpowder) and some pipe 
          with caps. Now doing this is very simple, Just get
          your pipe ( I recommend 1/2 ft of 3/4 inch Pipe) and 
          drill a hole in one of the caps. Fill the pipe up with
          Pyrodex and screw on both caps. insert a fuse through
          the hole that you drilled and you have a pipe Bomb.
          Pipe Bombs such as The one I recommened Can take out
          cars if placed in the right spot. They are very 
          Dangerous and if you blow off your hand dont come 
          and tell me.
