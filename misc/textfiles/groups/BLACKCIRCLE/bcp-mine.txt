                  Making a mine is very easy. here is what you 
                  need.

                        - Plank of wood.
                        - shotgun shell
                        - nails
                        - brick


                  nail nails around the brick and on to the board.
                  you should have a brick that has the 3 holes in 
                  the side of it. now make it so that a nail is 
                  sticking in the middle of one of the holes.
                  now put the shotgun shell so that the primer 
                  is on the nail. when pressure is applied to 
                  it the shotgun shell will go off sending shot
                  going everywhere. now all you have to do is go
                  and bury it.
