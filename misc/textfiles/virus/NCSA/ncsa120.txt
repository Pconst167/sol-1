Host Machine: PC compatibles.

Derived from: SYS virus.

     This is similar to the SYS virus, but it performs a hard disk format
on any Friday 13th after 1990. This virus, and its precursor virus both
still contain the 3.5" bug, so that they are easily detected on systems
using 3.5" drives. They are difficult to detect on other systems.
