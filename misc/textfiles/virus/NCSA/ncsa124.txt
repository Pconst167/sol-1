                    ภฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤฤู

Host Machine: PC compatibles.

Derived from: Yankee Doodle virus.

     This version of the Yankee Doodle virus does not contain the string
"VACSINA", and does not beep. When the CTRL-ALT-DEL combination is
pressed, the virus plays "Yankee Doodle" and then reboots. The virus
adds new PC/MS-DOS function callL 0xC5. This function call does many
different things, and also may be used as a virus cure.
